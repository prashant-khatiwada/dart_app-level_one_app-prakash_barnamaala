Prakash Barnamala (Nepali)
Version 1.0.04a26b

Prakash Barnamala is the mobile app to teach Nepali language to children and adults alike. It is developed in collaboration with Prakash Harinagare. Interesting and easy to learn, the app has images and audio files to make learning interesting. Easy to remember layouts and simple Devanagari/Nepali words to help the kids to remember Nepali. Includes exercises to practice the newly learnt alphabets and words.
